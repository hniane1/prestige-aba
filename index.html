Our Mission

At Prestige ABA Therapy, we are dedicated to empowering children with autism spectrum disorder and other developmental challenges to reach their full potential. Through evidence-based Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) therapy, we create individualized treatment plans that focus on building essential life skills and fostering independence.

What is ABA Therapy?

Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) is a scientifically validated approach to understanding behavior and how it is affected by the environment. ABA therapy applies this understanding to real situations with the goal of increasing helpful behaviors and decreasing harmful behaviors or those that affect learning.
